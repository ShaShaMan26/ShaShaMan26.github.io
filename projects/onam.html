About

One* Night at Miku's is an action focused FNaF fangame that combines classic sit-and-survive gameplay with Rougelike elements.

Features

  • Action spin on classic FNaF gameplay.
  • Rougelike-inspired upgrade/progression system (not in demo).
  • Engaging enemy AI.
  • Three difficulty modes.
  • Extensive play options.
